Pool FAQ

What does SOLO mean?

A valid block found by your miner is credited to your miner address, minus any configured pool fee. Rewards still need normal chain confirmations before they become payable.

Why do blocks or payments show empty?

This pool is new. Empty tables simply mean Miningcore has not recorded matching blocks or payments yet.

How are times shown?

Public times are displayed in Europe/Berlin format. Miningcore internally stores UTC timestamps.

Where can I get help?
